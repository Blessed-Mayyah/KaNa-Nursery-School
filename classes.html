KaNa Kindergarten and Day Care Centre Ltd

KaNa Kindergarten and Day Care Centre Ltd offers a range of classes designed to support the growth and development of young children. Our programs focus on holistic education, ensuring each child is nurtured academically, socially, and emotionally.

Classes at KaNa Kindergarten and Day Care Centre Ltd

KG1

Welcome to KG1, where our youngest learners begin their educational journey. We focus on play-based learning to develop social, emotional, and cognitive skills in a nurturing environment.

KG2

KG2 builds on the foundation set in KG1, introducing more structured learning activities. Our engaging curriculum encourages curiosity and supports the development of literacy and numeracy skills.

KG3

KG3 prepares children for their transition to primary school. We emphasize critical thinking, problem-solving, and independence, ensuring each child is ready for the next step in their education.
