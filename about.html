About Us

KaNa Kindergarten and Day Care Centre Ltd

KaNa Kindergarten and Day Care Centre Ltd is a Christian school founded by Sam Kafeero and Nalukwago Catherine. The name "Kana" is derived from their names: 'Ka' for Kafeero and 'Na' for Nalukwago. Our mission is to provide quality education and care in a nurturing environment that supports the growth and development of young children.

Vision

Trust: We believe in ourselves and in each other.

Mission

Perform: We endeavor to achieve our best and reach our full potential.

Values

Be proud: We take pride in ourselves, our school, and our achievements.

School Philosophy

Achieve full educational potential in an equitable, practical, and agreeable environment.

Play Ground

Our playground is designed with safety and fun in mind, providing a secure and stimulating environment where children can explore, play, and develop their motor skills.

Music and Dance

We offer engaging music and dance classes that help children express themselves creatively while developing rhythm, coordination, and confidence.

Safe Transportation

We ensure safe and reliable transportation for your children, with well-maintained vehicles and trained staff to provide peace of mind for parents.

Healthy Food

Our healthy food program provides nutritious and balanced meals, promoting healthy eating habits and ensuring your children have the energy they need for a full day of learning and play.
