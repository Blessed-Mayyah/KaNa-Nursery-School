Play Ground

Our playground is designed with safety and fun in mind, providing a secure and stimulating environment where children can explore, play, and develop their motor skills.

Music and Dance

We offer engaging music and dance classes that help children express themselves creatively while developing rhythm, coordination, and confidence.

Safe Transportation

We ensure safe and reliable transportation for your children, with well-maintained vehicles and trained staff to provide peace of mind for parents.

Healthy Food

Our healthy food program provides nutritious and balanced meals, promoting healthy eating habits and ensuring your children have the energy they need for a full day of learning and play.

Classes at KaNa Kindergarten and Day Care Centre Ltd

KG1

Welcome to KG1, where our youngest learners begin their educational journey. We focus on play-based learning to develop social, emotional, and cognitive skills in a nurturing environment.

KG2
